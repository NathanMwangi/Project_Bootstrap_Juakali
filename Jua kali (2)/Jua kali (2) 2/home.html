ABOUT US

  • Jua Kali is a web app designed to tackle unemployment in Kenya and across Africa. Created by Nathan Muchina, the platform aims to connect job seekers with opportunities, particularly in informal sectors, which are crucial in many African economies. Nathan was motivated to develop this website in response to the violence and unrest stemming from high unemployment rates in Kenya. By facilitating access to jobs and resources, Jua Kali seeks to empower individuals and contribute to economic stability in the region.

  • Jua Kali's mission is to reduce unemployment rates by providing a platform that connects entrepreneurs and job seekers, enabling access to job opportunities from anywhere in the world. By leveraging technology, the website aims to empower individuals to find work and grow their businesses, fostering economic growth and stability both locally and across Africa. Through this initiative, Jua Kali aspires to create a more inclusive job market and address the challenges posed by unemployment.

  • we offer

    Jua Kali envisions a financially stable Africa in the years to come by empowering individuals of all ages. Through its platform, the initiative aims to provide the tools and opportunities needed for people to thrive, ultimately contributing to a more prosperous and equitable continent. By fostering entrepreneurship and facilitating job access, Jua Kali seeks to create a brighter future and make Africa a better place for all.
